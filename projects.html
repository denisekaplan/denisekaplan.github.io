GetMoFit is a mobile application. Mo's a little chubby guy who needs your help getting in shape. In order for him to get healthy, you have to move. Mo's progress is dependent on a user's logged workouts as well as their daily steps imported from Apple's HealthKit. A progress bar increases and decrements based on your activity level and Mo gives you motivational messages (nice or sassy, depending on your settings) to keep you going.
