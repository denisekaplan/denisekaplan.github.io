GetMoFit, a mobile application that allows users to interact with Mo, a Tamagotchi-like creature, who gets fat if a user doesn't keep him in shape. In order to keep Mo in shape, you have to stay in shape! Mo's progress is dependent on a user's logged workouts as well as their daily steps imported from Apple's HealthKit. His progress will decrement as time between workouts increases.

Bo Jacobson, Andrew Diaz, and Denise Kaplan == B.A.D. Designs. We built this lightweight e-commerce site (with a custom content management system) for Scott Meskill, an artist and furniture designer in Southern California.
